9-11 Movies and TV Shows

A curated selection of notable movies and TV shows that explore the events, heroism, and aftermath of the September 11, 2001 attacks, highlighting real-time accounts, personal stories, and societal impacts as depicted in film and television.

Cloverfield

A group of friends venture deep into the streets of New York City on a rescue mission during a mysterious monster attack, set against the post-9/11 atmosphere.

DC 9/11: Time of Crisis

A dramatization focusing on the days immediately following the September 11 attacks, depicting the president's actions and response.

Diverted

The story of how 38 planes destined for New York were diverted to Gander, Newfoundland, where a small town hosted thousands of stranded passengers in the aftermath of 9/11.

Fahrenheit 9/11

Michael Moore's controversial documentary examining the U.S. government's actions after 9/11 and the ensuing wars, questioning political motives and consequences.

Flight 93

A dramatization of the heroic passengers on United Flight 93 who thwarted a terrorist attack by retaking control of their hijacked plane.

Into the Fire

The story of Walter Hartwig, a lieutenant in the New York City Harbor Unit, who confronts what truly matters in life after the tragedy of 9/11.

New York

A contemporary story centering on three friends discovering life in New York City, whose lives and world change after the events of September 11.

Reign Over Me

A man who lost his family in the 9/11 attacks rekindles a friendship with his old college roommate, seeking healing and recovery from his grief.

The Love We Make

In the aftermath of 9/11, Paul McCartney organizes a benefit concert through New York City streets to honor victims and aid recovery efforts.

Tiger Cruise

Following the 9/11 attacks, a naval aircraft carrier with civilians onboard shifts into combat mode, exploring themes of duty and sacrifice.

Twin Towers

Documenting the lives and bravery of two brothers, a firefighter and a police officer, during the September 11 attacks in New York City.

The Path to 9/11

A TV miniseries depicting the events leading up to the terrorist attacks on September 11, 2001, focusing on political and intelligence failures.

United 93

A real-time dramatization of the hijacking of United Airlines Flight 93 and the passengers' efforts to foil a terrorist attack on the U.S. Capitol.

WTC View

A young man struggles to find a new roommate and maintain emotional balance after placing an ad the day before the 9/11 attacks in New York City.

9/11 (2017)

Five people find themselves trapped in an elevator in the World Trade Center during the attacks, portraying their struggle and courage.

9/11: Fifteen Years Later

A reflective documentary revisiting the experiences of FDNY firefighters who were embedded at Ground Zero during the 9/11 attacks.
