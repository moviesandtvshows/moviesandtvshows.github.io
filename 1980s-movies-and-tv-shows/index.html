1980s Movies and TV Shows

A curated list of iconic and quintessential movies and TV shows from the 1980s, capturing the cultural essence and entertainment of the decade.

The A-Team

A group of ex-US Army Special Forces personnel work as soldiers of fortune while on the run from the military for a crime they didn't commit.

Back to the Future

A teenager travels back in time and accidentally interferes with his parents' meeting, risking his own existence.

Batman (1989)

The dark and mysterious Batman faces off against the villainous Joker in Gotham City.

Beetlejuice

A recently deceased couple hire a rambunctious ghost to help scare away the new living inhabitants of their home.

Cheers

Set in a Boston bar, this TV sitcom follows the lives of its regular patrons and staff.

Diff'rent Strokes

Two African-American boys from Harlem are adopted by a wealthy white businessman and his daughter.

Family Ties

A sitcom focused on the family dynamics between liberal parents and their conservative children in the 1980s.

Ferris Bueller's Day Off

A high school wise guy is determined to have a day off from school, despite what the principal thinks.

Full House

A widowed father raises his three daughters with the help of a close friend and his brother-in-law.

Ghostbusters

Three parapsychologists start a ghost-catching business in New York City.

Gremlins

A young man inadvertently unleashes a horde of mischievous creatures in his small town.

Knight Rider

A high-tech modern crime fighter fights injustice with an advanced, artificially intelligent car.

Magnum, P.I.

A private investigator in Hawaii takes on various cases with charm and wit.

Miami Vice

Two undercover cops in Miami tackle crime while living the high life.

Murder, She Wrote

A mystery writer uses her detective skills to solve real-life murders.

Police Squad!

A parody detective series featuring Leslie Nielsen as the oblivious Lieutenant Frank Drebin.

The Breakfast Club

Five high school students from different cliques spend a Saturday in detention together.

The Cosby Show

A sitcom about an upper-middle-class African American family navigating daily life.

The Dukes of Hazzard

The adventures of the Duke cousins as they outsmart the corrupt county authorities.

The Princess Bride

A fairy tale adventure about love, adventure, and heroism.

Transformers (TV Show)

An animated series about robots that can transform into vehicles battling for control.
