2000s Movies and TV Shows

A curated alphabetical selection of memorable movies and TV shows from the 2000s decade, showcasing a mix of genres and popular titles that defined the era.

Alias

A thrilling spy drama following Sydney Bristow, a double agent navigating espionage and deception.

Buffy the Vampire Slayer

A cult TV series about Buffy, a young woman fighting vampires and supernatural forces in high school.

Donnie Darko

A psychologically intense film exploring time travel and fate through a troubled teenager's visions.

The Dark Knight

A critically acclaimed superhero film featuring Batman's battle against the anarchist Joker.

Elf

A beloved Christmas comedy about a man raised as an elf in the North Pole discovering his true identity.

Finding Nemo

An animated adventure following a clownfish searching across the ocean to find his lost son.

Gladiator

An epic historical drama chronicling a betrayed general's quest for vengeance and justice in Rome.

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

A classic 2000s romantic comedy about a bet-driven relationship that turns unexpectedly real.

Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The epic conclusion to the fantasy trilogy where heroes battle to save Middle-earth from darkness.

Mamma Mia!

A musical film featuring ABBA songs and a bride-to-be searching for her father among three potential men.

The Office (US)

A comedic mockumentary about the everyday lives of office employees at a paper company.

Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl

A swashbuckling adventure featuring pirates, curses, and high seas escapades led by Captain Jack Sparrow.

Six Feet Under

A darkly comedic drama about a family running a funeral home and confronting life and death issues.

Spider-Man 2

A superhero sequel continuing Peter Parker's struggles balancing his powers and personal life.

The Sopranos

An influential TV drama about a mob boss managing crime and his complicated family dynamics.

Veronica Mars

A teen noir series following a sharp high school detective solving mysteries in her town.

WALL-E

An animated sci-fi story about a waste-collecting robot discovering love and humanity’s future.

X-Men

A superhero film centered on mutant-powered individuals fighting for acceptance and peace.

24

An intense real-time action TV series chronicling counter-terrorist agent Jack Bauer's missions.

The West Wing

A political drama depicting the personal and professional lives of the U.S. President and staff.

The Wire

A gritty crime drama exploring Baltimore’s drug trade, institutions, and law enforcement.
