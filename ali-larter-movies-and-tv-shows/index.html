Biographical Overview of Ali Larter

Alison Elizabeth "Ali" Larter (born February 28, 1976, in Cherry Hill, New Jersey) is an American actress and former model. She first gained attention playing Clear Rivers in the horror franchise "Final Destination" and later starred in TV series like "Heroes," where she played the dual roles of Niki Sanders and Tracy Strauss. Larter has worked across genres in film and television, also notable for roles in “Varsity Blues,” “Legally Blonde,” and the “Resident Evil” series. She continues to act and produce works in Hollywood, blending mainstream and independent projects.

  • Legally Blonde (2001)

  • Resident Evil: The Final Chapter (2016)
