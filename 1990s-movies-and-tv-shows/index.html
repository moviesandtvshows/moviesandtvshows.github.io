1990s Movies and TV Shows

A selection of popular movies and TV shows from the 1990s, reflecting diverse genres and iconic cultural moments of the decade.

Beverly Hills, 90210

A TV series following the lives of friends navigating adolescence and adulthood in wealthy Beverly Hills.

Cape Fear

A thriller about a convicted rapist stalking the family of the lawyer who defended him.

Edward Scissorhands

A fantasy romance about a gentle man with scissor hands finding love and acceptance.

Goodfellas

The true story of Henry Hill's life in the mafia, highlighting his relationships and crime life.

Gremlins 2: The New Batch

The mischievous gremlins take over a high-tech NYC skyscraper, causing chaos.

Hook

An adult Peter Pan returns to Neverland to rescue his children from Captain Hook.

Kindergarten Cop

A tough cop poses as a kindergarten teacher to infiltrate a criminal's life.

Look Who's Talking Too

A family comedy focusing on sibling rivalry and family life with a new baby on the way.

Mr. Bean

A comedy TV series about the bumbling and resourceful character Mr. Bean facing everyday challenges.

Pretty Woman

A romantic drama about a rich entrepreneur falling in love with a prostitute he hires for social events.

Rock-A-Doodle

A boy turned into a cat teams up with barnyard animals to find a rooster who can raise the sun.

Se7en

Two detectives hunt a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his motives.

The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air

A streetwise Philadelphia youth is sent to live with wealthy relatives in Bel-Air.

The NeverEnding Story II: The Next Chapter

A young boy enters a fantasy world through a magical book while searching for his distant father.

The Rescuers Down Under

Animated adventure of two rescue agents saving a boy and rare eagle from a poacher in Australia.

The Silence of the Lambs

An FBI cadet seeks help from a cannibalistic killer to catch another serial murderer.

The Silver Chair

In this TV series, Eustace and Jill Pole search for a missing prince in Narnia by Aslan’s command.

War of the Birds

Animals in a forest unite to fight an evil bird threatening their peaceful home.

Welcome to the Dollhouse

A dark comedy about the struggles of an awkward teen girl as she deals with family and school.

This Boy's Life

A coming-of-age drama about a boy living with his abusive stepfather in the 1950s.
