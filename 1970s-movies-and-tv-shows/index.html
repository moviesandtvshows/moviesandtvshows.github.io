1970s Movies and TV Shows

Explore a selection of iconic movies and TV shows from the 1970s, showcasing a variety of genres and cultural impact from this memorable decade in entertainment history.

Adam-12

A TV series that follows the daily activities of two Los Angeles police officers, showcasing 1970s law enforcement life.

All My Children

A long-running soap opera centered around the lives and dramas of residents in the fictional town of Pine Valley.

Bonanza

Classic western TV series focusing on the Cartwright family in the 19th century American West.

Columbo

Mystery TV series featuring a shrewd but seemingly absent-minded detective who solves complex crimes.

Dallas

Drama series chronicling the power struggles and family drama of a wealthy Texas oil family.

Diff'rent Strokes

Comedy TV show about a wealthy white family adopting two African-American boys from Harlem.

Gunsmoke

One of the longest-running western TV shows depicting life in Dodge City in the late 19th century.

Happy Days

Sitcom set in the 1950s focusing on the Cunningham family and their adventures, including the iconic character Fonzie.

Hee Haw

Country-themed variety show featuring country music and country comedy sketches.

Hawaii Five-O

Police procedural TV show about an elite state police unit in Hawaii solving crimes.

Ironside

Crime drama about a wheelchair-bound detective solving cases with his team.

Marcus Welby, M.D.

Medical drama that follows the professional and personal lives of Dr. Marcus Welby and his colleagues.

Mannix

Detective TV series focusing on private investigator Joe Mannix and his cases.

Mary Tyler Moore Show

Sitcom about a single woman working as a TV news producer, breaking new ground in feminist television.

Medical Center

Drama centered on the staff and patients at a large university hospital.

M*A*S*H

Comedy-drama series set during the Korean War focusing on a mobile army surgical hospital.

The Carol Burnett Show

Variety/sketch comedy show starring Carol Burnett, known for its humor and iconic performances.

The Dean Martin Show

Variety show hosted by Dean Martin featuring music, comedy sketches, and celebrity guests.

The Doris Day Show

Sitcom starring Doris Day as a widow starting a new life and career on a ranch.

The Flip Wilson Show

Comedy sketch series featuring Flip Wilson and his variety of characters and sketches.

The F.B.I.

Crime drama series focusing on real cases tackled by the Federal Bureau of Investigation.

The Jeffersons

Sitcom following an affluent African American family who moved to a luxury apartment.

The Love Boat

Romantic and comedic tales of the passengers and crew aboard a cruise ship.

The Mod Squad

Crime drama about three young undercover cops who use their youth culture to solve cases.

Three's Company

Sitcom about two women and one man sharing an apartment and the resulting misunderstandings.

Taxi

Comedy-drama about the employees of a New York City taxicab company aiming for better lives.
