About Billkin

Billkin Putthipong Assaratanakul, known simply as Billkin, is a talented Thai actor, singer, and model born on October 8, 1999, in Bangkok, Thailand. Rising to prominence through his captivating roles in Thai dramas and his music career, Billkin has become a popular and respected figure in the Southeast Asian entertainment industry. He is celebrated for his performances in "I Told Sunset About You" and the award-winning film "How to Make Millions Before Grandma Dies."

Movies

  • How to Make Millions Before Grandma Dies (2024) – Billkin stars as M, portraying a young man caring for his terminally ill grandmother while hoping to inherit her fortune in this acclaimed Thai drama.
  • The Red Envelope (2025) – Billkin plays the main role Menn in this upcoming film, continuing his trajectory in impactful cinema.
  • Brother of the Year (2018) – Appears in a guest role in this popular Thai comedy-drama focusing on family dynamics.
  • I Told Sunset About You: The Documentary (2020) – Explores the making and success of the series that brought Billkin international fame.
  • My Ambulance (2019, Soundtrack Contribution) – While not a movie, his contributions to the soundtrack added to the project’s success.

TV Series

  • I Promised You the Moon (2021) – A sequel where Billkin reprises his role as Teh, further delving into complex relationships and growth.
  • My Ambulance (2019) – Features as Tao, a supporting role in the popular Thai medical drama series.
  • One Year: 365 Wan Ban Chun Ban Tur (2019) – Billkin appears as Porsche in this drama series.
  • Please... Siang Riak Winyan (2017) – Billkin’s earlier supporting role contributing to his development as an actor.
