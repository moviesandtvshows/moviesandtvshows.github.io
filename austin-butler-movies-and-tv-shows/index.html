About Austin Butler

Austin Robert Butler is an American actor and singer recognized for his versatility and captivating performances. He gained fame through TV roles in shows like "The Carrie Diaries" and later achieved critical acclaim starring as Elvis Presley in Baz Luhrmann's 2022 biopic "Elvis." Butler has also appeared in major films such as "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood" and "Dune: Part Two." His dedication to his craft has earned him several prestigious awards and nominations.

Movies

  • Elvis (2022) Butler portrays Elvis Presley in this highly acclaimed biopic, a role that earned him Golden Globe and BAFTA awards and an Academy Award nomination.
  • Once Upon a Time in Hollywood (2019) Played Tex Watson, a member of the Manson Family, in Quentin Tarantinos nostalgic crime drama.
  • The Dead Dont Die (2019) A comedic horror film where he is part of a star-studded ensemble dealing with a zombie outbreak.
  • Yoga Hosers (2016) A quirky comedy-horror film where he stars alongside other young talents.
  • Aliens in the Attic (2009) Family comedy featuring Butler in a supporting role involving a battle with invading aliens.
