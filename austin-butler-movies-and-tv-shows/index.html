Austin Butler Movies And TV Shows

Explore the diverse and acclaimed filmography of Austin Butler, an American actor known for his standout roles in movies and television series spanning drama, sci-fi, and biographical genres.

Aliens in the Attic

A 2009 family sci-fi comedy film featuring Austin Butler as Jake Pearson.

Caught Stealing

An upcoming 2025 film where Butler stars as Hank Thompson; currently in post-production.

Dune: Part Two

A 2024 science fiction epic film featuring Butler as Feyd-Rautha Harkonnen.

Dude

A 2018 drama film where Butler plays Thomas Daniels.

Elvis

The 2022 biographical musical film starring Austin Butler as Elvis Presley, earning him wide acclaim.

Eddington

A 2025 film with Butler playing Vernon Jefferson Peak.

Masters of the Air

A 2024 war drama miniseries starring Butler as Major Gale "Buck" Cleven.

Once Upon a Time... in Hollywood

The Bikeriders

A 2023 drama film starring Butler as Benny.

The Carrie Diaries

A 2013-2014 TV series where Butler played Sebastian Kydd.

The Dead Don't Die

A 2019 zombie comedy horror film with Butler in the role of Jack.

The Intruders

A 2015 thriller starring Butler as Noah Henry.

The Shannara Chronicles

A 2016-2017 fantasy TV series in which Butler portrayed Wil Ohmsford.

Yoga Hosers

A 2016 comedy horror film with Butler as Hunter Calloway.
