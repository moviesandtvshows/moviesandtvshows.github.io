Adam Scott movies and tv shows

Biographical Overview of Adam Scott

Adam Paul Scott (born April 3, 1973) is an American actor and comedian best known for his role as Ben Wyatt in the NBC sitcom Parks and Recreation (2010–2015). Born and raised in Santa Cruz, California, Scott honed his acting skills at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts and built a diverse career with roles in comedy, drama, and voice acting. His notable works include Step Brothers, Party Down, The Good Place, and critically acclaimed performances in Big Little Lies and Apple TV+'s Severance.

Movies featuring Adam Scott

  • Step Brothers (2008)
    Scott plays Derek Huff, an obnoxious brother-in-law in this comedy about two adults forced to live together as stepbrothers.
  • The Aviator (2004)
    He appears in this biopic about aviation pioneer Howard Hughes, set during Hollywood's golden age.
  • Krampus (2015)
    A horror-comedy where Scott plays a father dealing with supernatural forces on Christmas.
  • The Secret Life of Walter Mitty (2013)
    Scott appears in this adventure comedy about an ordinary man who embarks on an extraordinary global journey.
  • My Blind Brother (2016)
    A comedy-drama where Scott plays a competitive brother dealing with family issues.

TV Series starring or featuring Adam Scott

  • Parks and Recreation (2010–2015)
    Scott gained fame as Ben Wyatt, an earnest state auditor and love interest of Leslie Knope in the comedy about small-town government.
  • Severance (2022–present)
    In this critically praised Apple TV+ sci-fi thriller, Scott plays Mark Scout, an employee with split consciousness between work and personal life.
  • Party Down (2009–2010, 2023)
    A cult comedy about a group of aspiring actors and writers working for a catering company, with Scott as Henry Pollard.
  • The Good Place (2016–2018)
    Scott portrays Trevor, a sarcastic demon, in this philosophical comedy series about the afterlife.
  • Big Little Lies (2017–2019)
    He plays Ed Mackenzie, the supportive husband of Reese Witherspoon’s character, in this drama about dark secrets in a wealthy community.
