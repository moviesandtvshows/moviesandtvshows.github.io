Alan Ritchson movies and tv shows

Biographical Overview of Alan Ritchson

Alan Michael Ritchson (born November 28, 1982) is an American actor, model, and singer. He gained early recognition as Aquaman on the TV series Smallville and later for his role as Thad Castle on the Spike TV sitcom Blue Mountain State. Ritchson expanded his career with notable film roles including Raphael in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles reboot, Gloss in The Hunger Games: Catching Fire, and as the lead in the Amazon Prime series Reacher.

Movies starring Alan Ritchson

  • Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2014)
    Ritchson portrays Raphael, one of the turtle brothers skilled in combat and known for his rebellious nature.
  • The Hunger Games: Catching Fire (2013)
    Plays Gloss, District 1’s strong and attractive victor, competing in the deadly games.
  • Fast X (2023)
    Appears in the tenth installment of the fast-paced action franchise focusing on street racing and heists.
  • Dark Web: Cicada 3301 (2021)
    Ritchson directed, co-wrote, and starred in this action-comedy thriller about secretive hacker groups.
  • The Wedding Ringer (2015)
    A comedy where Ritchson plays a best man-for-hire helping a groom without friends.

TV Series featuring Alan Ritchson

  • Reacher (2022–present)
    Lead role as Jack Reacher, a former military policeman who solves dangerous cases, acclaimed for action and storytelling.
  • Blue Mountain State (2010–2012)
    Comedy series where he stars as Thad Castle, the arrogant and competitive captain of a college football team.
  • Titans (2018–2021)
    Plays Hawk, a superhero with enhanced abilities, part of the DC Universe series.
  • Smallville (2005–2010)
    Portrayed Aquaman/Arthur Curry in guest appearances throughout the series.
  • Blood Drive (2017)
    Leads this cult SyFy series about a deadly cross-country car race.
