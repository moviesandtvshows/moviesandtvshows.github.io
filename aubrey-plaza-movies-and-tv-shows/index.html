About Aubrey Plaza

Aubrey Plaza is an American actress, comedian, and producer known for her deadpan humor and unique comedic style. Rising to fame with her role as April Ludgate on the TV series "Parks and Recreation," Plaza has built a diverse career in both television and film, gaining critical acclaim for her work in indie films and engaging performances on screen.

Movies

  • Ingrid Goes West – Aubrey stars as Ingrid, an obsessed social media follower who moves to Los Angeles to befriend her Instagram idol, exploring themes of loneliness and the impact of social media.
  • Safety Not Guaranteed – Plaza plays a quirky supermarket employee who helps investigate a classified ad seeking a time travel companion, blending comedy and indie charm.
  • The To Do List – A coming-of-age comedy where Aubrey portrays a rebellious friend helping the protagonist explore her teenage desires and awkwardness.
  • Child’s Play (2019) – Aubrey features in this horror reboot about a high-tech doll that turns violent, showing her versatility beyond comedy roles.
  • Black Bear – A psychological thriller where she plays a filmmaker navigating complex relationships, delivering an intense and layered performance.
