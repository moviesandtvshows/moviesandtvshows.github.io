Biographical Overview of Adam Brody

Adam Jared Brody (born December 15, 1979) is an American actor known for his breakout role as Seth Cohen on the Fox television series The O.C. (2003–2007). Brody has appeared in several films and TV series, showcasing his versatility in both comedic and dramatic roles. His notable film credits include Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005), Jennifer's Body (2009), and Shazam! (2019). Brody has also acted in and produced TV series like StartUp (2016–2018) and received critical acclaim for his role in the Netflix series Nobody Wants This (2024).

Movies featuring Adam Brody

  • Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005)
    Brody plays a supporting role in this action-comedy about a married couple who are both secret assassins unaware of each other's identity.
  • Jennifer’s Body (2009)
    A horror-comedy where Brody plays the love interest of a high school girl possessed by a demon.
  • Scream 4 (2011)
    Brody appears in this slasher film as Deputy Hoss, part of the new generation facing the Ghostface killer.
  • The Oranges (2011)
    A romantic comedy-drama featuring Brody as a businessman whose daughter falls in love with a family friend.
  • Shazam! (2019)
    Brody plays a superhero movie role as the character Freddy Freeman’s father in this DC Comics film.

TV Series starring or featuring Adam Brody
