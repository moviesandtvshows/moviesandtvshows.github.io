Biographical Overview of Alexandra Daddario

Alexandra Anna Daddario (born March 16, 1986, in New York City) is an American actress renowned for her captivating screen presence and versatile performances. She gained fame as Annabeth Chase in the "Percy Jackson" film series and earned critical acclaim for her role in the HBO series "True Detective." Daddario has appeared in various genres, from horror to comedy, and was nominated for a Primetime Emmy for her performance in "The White Lotus."

  • San Andreas (2015)
    Plays Blake Gaines, the daughter of a rescue pilot navigating the aftermath of a massive earthquake upending California.
  • Baywatch (2017)
    Features as Summer Quinn, a lifeguard involved in comedic and action-packed rescues.
