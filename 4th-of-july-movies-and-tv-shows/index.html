4th of July Movies and TV Shows

Explore a curated list of movies and TV shows ideal for celebrating the 4th of July, featuring patriotic themes, American history, summer vibes, and iconic moments that capture the spirit of Independence Day.

Air Force One

A tense thriller where the US President fights hijackers onboard Air Force One, highlighting themes of leadership and patriotism.

Armageddon

An action-packed disaster film about a team sent to stop an asteroid from hitting Earth, combining high stakes and American heroism.

Born on the Fourth of July

The biographical story of Ron Kovic, a Vietnam War veteran who becomes a political activist, reflecting on freedom and sacrifice.

Captain America: The First Avenger

The origin of a superhero who embodies American ideals of justice and courage during World War II.

Clear and Present Danger

CIA analyst Jack Ryan faces an illegal war against a Colombian drug cartel, highlighting governmental and patriotic tensions.

Despicable Me 2

A family-friendly animated sequel with fun and lighthearted adventures ideal for a relaxed holiday watch.

Forrest Gump

An epic drama portraying decades of American history through the eyes of an endearing man, evoking nostalgia and patriotism.

Hamilton

A contemporary musical retelling of Alexander Hamilton’s life emphasizing American history and independence.

Independence Day

Iconic sci-fi action film featuring an alien invasion that coincides with July 4th, complete with legendary speeches and explosive battles.

Jaws

Thriller set over a 4th of July weekend in a beach community, where a killer shark terrorizes local residents.

Men in Black

A sci-fi comedy about secret agents policing extraterrestrial activity on Earth with American urban flair.

Minions: The Rise of Gru

A fun, animated family film with mischievous characters providing light entertainment for the holiday.

National Treasure

An adventure film involving a treasure hunt linked to Founding Fathers’ secrets and American landmarks.

Saving Private Ryan

A World War II epic following U.S. soldiers on a dangerous mission, emphasizing sacrifice and patriotism.

Southland Tales

A satirical sci-fi film exploring America’s future and identity against a backdrop of chaos.

Spider-Man: Far From Home

Superhero action with themes of responsibility and courage, capturing modern American pop culture.

Stranger Things – “The Battle of Starcourt” (S3, E8)

A nostalgic 80s sci-fi series finale set during July 4th, featuring fireworks and a climactic showdown.

The Great Gatsby

A drama about the Roaring Twenties capturing American dreams and disillusionment.

The Sandlot

A family comedy centered on childhood summer adventures and iconic 4th of July fireworks scenes.

Transformers: Dark of the Moon

A sci-fi action film showcasing battles with a patriotic undertone set around American defense themes.

War of the Worlds

A thrilling alien invasion story emphasizing survival and human resilience during a chaotic period.
