2010 Movies and TV Shows

A selection of notable movies and TV shows released in 2010, showcasing a variety of genres and stories from that year.

127 Hours

A mountain climber becomes trapped under a boulder while canyoneering alone near Moab, Utah and resorts to desperate measures in order to survive.

Black Swan

A psychological thriller about a dancer's descent into madness while competing for the lead role in Swan Lake.

Buried

A U.S. truck driver working in Iraq wakes to find he is buried alive inside a coffin and must race against time to escape.

Despicable Me

A supervillain adopts three girls as part of a grand scheme but finds his heart changing along the way.

Frozen

Three skiers stranded on a chairlift are forced to make life-or-death choices in a chilling survival story.

Green Zone

A rogue U.S. Army officer hunts for Weapons of Mass Destruction in an unstable Iraq amid deception and danger.

How to Train Your Dragon

A young Viking forms an unlikely friendship with a feared dragon, changing his village forever.

Incendies

Twins journey to the Middle East to discover their family history and fulfill their mother's last wishes.

Inception

A skilled thief invades people's dreams to steal secrets but faces his own mind-bending mission.

The Expendables

A CIA operative hires mercenaries to take down a Latin dictator and a rogue agent.

The Karate Kid

A young boy learns Kung Fu from a master to face his bullies after moving to China with his mother.

The King's Speech

The story of King George VI's struggle to overcome his stammer with the help of a speech therapist.

The Last Airbender

A young Avatar must master all elements to save the world from the Fire Nation's tyranny.

The Social Network

A dramatized story about the founding of Facebook and the legal battles that followed.

The Tourist

A mysterious woman approaches a tourist on a train in Venice for an unusual reason.

The Town

A group of Boston bank robbers face complications when one of them falls in love with a hostage.

The Way Back

Siberian gulag escapees embark on a 4,000-mile journey to freedom in India.

Toy Story 3

The toys face their biggest adventure yet as they confront life beyond their owner’s childhood.

True Grit

A determined young girl hires a tough U.S. Marshal to track down her father's murderer.

Undisputed 3: Redemption

Boyka fights in the first-ever inter-prison tournament despite a knee injury.
