2007 Movies and TV Shows

A curated alphabetical list of notable movies and TV shows released in 2007, showcasing a variety of genres and memorable titles from that year.

28 Weeks Later

Six months after the rage virus devastated Great Britain, survivors in London face new threats, as the US Army tries to secure a safe zone.

American Gangster

A New York cop takes on Harlem drug lord Frank Lucas in a partly biographical crime drama.

A teenager under house arrest suspects his neighbor is a serial killer as he spies on the neighborhood.

Enchanted

A fairy tale princess is transported to modern-day New York City, blending animation and live-action.

The Fantastic Four face the powerful Silver Surfer and planet-eater Galactus to save Earth.

Harry and his friends face increasing threats as Hogwarts comes under tight control amid the rise of Lord Voldemort.

Into the Wild

The true story of Christopher McCandless, who abandons society to live in the Alaskan wilderness.

National Treasure 2: Book of Secrets

Nicolas Cage returns as a treasure hunter uncovering historical secrets and conspiracies.

Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End

Jack Sparrow’s crew battles to rescue him and confront enemies controlling the seas.

Ratatouille

A rat with culinary talent forms an alliance with a young chef in a Paris restaurant.

Resident Evil: Extinction

The post-apocalyptic saga continues as survivors fight zombies across the desert wasteland.

Shrek the Third

Shrek embarks on a quest to find the rightful heir to the throne of Far Far Away.

Shooter

An expert marksman is forced to stop a presidential assassination plot while evading a conspiracy against him.

Spider-Man 3

Peter Parker faces new villains and inner conflicts after bonding with a mysterious black entity.

Superbad

Two high school seniors navigate friendship and chaos during a party-filled night.

The Bourne Ultimatum

Jason Bourne continues his search for identity while evading assassination attempts.

The Mist

Residents trapped in a grocery store face dangers from mysterious creatures lurking in a fog.

There Will Be Blood

A story of ambition, greed, and madness centered on an early 20th-century oil prospector.

Transformers

Alien robots Autobots and Decepticons battle on Earth, with a teenager caught in the middle.

Zodiac
