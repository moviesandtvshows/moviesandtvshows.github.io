50 Cent Movies and TV Shows

Explore an collection of movies and TV shows featuring Curtis "50 Cent" Jackson, showcasing his diverse roles ranging from crime thrillers to executive producer credits in popular series and films.

13 (2010)

A direct-to-video thriller where 50 Cent plays Jimmy, embroiled in crime and deception.

All Things Fall Apart (2011)

50 Cent appears as Deon in this direct-to-video film focusing on sports, family, and personal challenges.

Before I Self Destruct (2009)

Directed and written by 50 Cent, who also stars as Clarence in this drama about crime and revenge.

Blood Out (2011)

50 Cent serves as executive producer and acts as Hardwick in this action thriller dealing with gang rivalry.

Caught in the Crossfire (2010)

An executive-produced direct-to-video crime action film where 50 Cent plays Tino.

Den of Thieves (2018)

A heist action film featuring 50 Cent as Enson Levoux, delving into crime and law enforcement conflicts.

Escape Plan (2013)

50 Cent plays Hush, starring in this action thriller about breaking out of maximum security prisons.

Escape Plan 2: Hades (2018)

A direct-to-video sequel with 50 Cent reprising his role as Hush in more prison escape action.

Escape Plan: The Extractors (2019)

Another direct-to-video sequel where 50 Cent returns as Hush in an extraction mission.

Expend4bles (2023)

An action film where 50 Cent plays Easy Day, part of a team undertaking a dangerous mission.

Get Rich or Die Tryin' (2005)

A semi-autobiographical film where 50 Cent stars as Marcus, chronicling the struggles of a young rapper.

Home of the Brave (2006)

50 Cent portrays Jamal Aiken in this war drama about soldiers returning from Afghanistan.

Righteous Kill (2008)

Crime thriller featuring 50 Cent as Spider, acting alongside stars like Robert De Niro and Al Pacino.

Southpaw (2015)

50 Cent appears as Jordan Mains in this sports drama centered on boxing and personal redemption.

Spy (2015)

A cameo role by 50 Cent in this action-comedy spy film.

The Prince (2014)

Direct-to-video crime drama where 50 Cent plays The Pharmacy involved in mob conflicts.

Twelve (2010)

A drama about drug addiction in affluent New York City, with 50 Cent as Lionel.

Vengeance (2006 & 2014)

50 Cent plays Black in both the original and 2014 direct-to-video versions of this action film.

Power (2014-2020) [TV Series]

50 Cent stars as Kanan and is an executive producer of this critically acclaimed crime drama series.

For Life (2020–2021) [TV Series]

As Cassius Dawkins, 50 Cent also serves as executive producer in this drama inspired by a true story.
