Biographical Overview of 50 Cent

Curtis James Jackson III, known professionally as 50 Cent, is an American rapper, actor, producer, and entrepreneur born on July 6, 1975, in Queens, New York. He began his music career in the late 1990s and rose to worldwide fame with his 2003 debut album "Get Rich or Die Tryin'." Over the years, 50 Cent has diversified his career into acting and business, founding G-Unit Records and starring in the popular TV series "Power."
